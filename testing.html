Product Summary

This 36-gal. Better Built® HD Heavy-Duty Steel Vertical Transfer Tank is designed for easy transport of non-flammable liquids to the jobsite. Each tank is 100% quality inspected and undergoes a dual pressure and submersion leak test to ensure top performance. 45 1/4in.L x 11in.W x 17in.H (with lifting eyes).
